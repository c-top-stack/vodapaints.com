BOARD of Directors

Our BOARD of Directors

Rotimi Aluko

Chairman/Managing Director

Chairman/Managing Director, a Fellow of the National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria and a holder of an MBA from Lagos State University, has over twenty three years experience in the building industry manufacturing sector particularly in the paints and cement sub-sectors. He also attended several senior management and leadership courses in Nigeria and abroad including some at Insead, Ashridge and Lafarge Knowledge Campus in France. He has occupied various managerial positions prominent among which are the headship of Sales and Marketing Teams of Sandtex Brand of Paint, Elephant Cement and Cement Import and Marketing Operation of Sandtex Cement.

Olujimi Ogunmokun

Finance Director

Olujimi Ogunmokun, Finance Director, holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting from the University of Benin and is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He started his accounting career from the audit firm of Akintola Williams and Partners in 1983 from where he moved into manufacturing sector in the late 1980s when he joined the Oodua Group as an accountant and later moved on to West African Portland Cement Plc where he rose to become the Chief Accountant. He joined the Board of Voda Paints in 2005.

Akinboro, AbiodunJimoh
